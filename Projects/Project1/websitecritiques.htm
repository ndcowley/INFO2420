Web Site Critiques

Trader Joes http://www.traderjoes.com/

  • My first impression of the site was that there is a lot going on and I wasn't sure what I was supposed to look at first.
  • The site organizes the flow of information by having a sidbar on the left hand side with links to main pages within the site.
  • The site structure makes sense for the most part but I will admit there is a little too much text to read. I don't want a lot of text. It should be a quick look.
  • I think the graphics enhance the site. For trader joes you'd expect to see pictures of food on the website so it accomplishes that very well.
  • The loading time for the homepage was very quick. I think it's because most of the information is static data so there's not a ton of server load.
  • The font sizes and background colors aren't too distracting. The font is nice and pleasing and easy to read.
  • The site has two naviation bars. It's got a side bar with 4 links then a hamburger menu with way too much going on. It has so many links in the hamburger menu you start to get overwhelmed.
  • The site did have a theme I felt. Most of the background colors are real earthy colors. Light browns, mustard yellows, etc.
  • The site does a great job with the responsive design. I tried it on desktop, tablet and mobile view and it all looked really good.
  • It was unusual that there were 2 navigation menus. I guess there was just so much content it didn't do the best at letting you know where to go first or how to find something quick.

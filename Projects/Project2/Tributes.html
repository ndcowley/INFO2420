Bessie Anderson's Experience

My name is Bessie Anderson, and I currently live in a retirement community that has a prohibition on pets. I really wanted to have a small dog that I could take out for walks, but I thought it was not possible. Then I heard about Rent A Pet and was excited to give it a try.

I found a lovable basset hound and met her when she was delivered to my apartment one Saturday morning. We went for a long walk in the nearby park and had a wonderful time. The great Rent A Pet staff came and picked her up two hours later, so I didn’t even have to worry about feeding or watering "Sophie."

We now have a regular appointment every Saturday, weather permitting. I know I look forward to my weekly walk in the park, and I imagine Sophie likes it as well. Thanks for giving me something to look forward to each week!

Yours, Bessie Anderson

Jason Hutchingson's Story

I’m a single college guy and live in an apartment with three other guys. I always wanted a large breed of dog to be a companion for running or playing catch in the park. With the help of Rent A Pet, I found Kamaikaze, a German Shepherd.

Kazi loves to run in the summer and even cross-country ski in the winter. His favorite activity is playing Frisbee and running through an obstacle course. I always attract attention in the park when we go there. I even met my fiancée, Janice, when she joined Kazi and me in a heated game of keep-away.

One day, I hope to have a home with a yard where I can keep my own dog. Until then, I can still enjoy the companionship of a good dog. Thanks to Rent A Pet!

Sincerely, Jason Hutchingson

Michael Pinette's Unique Companions

My business requires me to travel quite a bit, so owning a pet of my own would be challenging. I’d constantly have to find a pet sitter or pay for boarding. I also like to surprise and shock my friends with unusual pets. Rent A Pet is the perfect solution for my lifestyle.

I have rented a snake, a Saint Bernard, a pot-bellied pig, and an iguana. My nurturing skills are sadly lacking—I can’t even keep a houseplant alive! With Rent A Pet, I can enjoy exotic pets without worrying about keeping them healthy and happy.

Thanks for adding spice to my life!

Michael Pinette, Alpine

Melinda Morrison's Dream Come True

As a young girl, I developed a love of horses, but I grew up in the city and never had the chance to own one. I was delighted to find Rent A Pet. They have several horses to choose from for riders of different skill levels, and they even deliver the horse to the trail where I want to ride.

Because I can request the same horse each time, I’ve become very comfortable with my riding skills and the abilities of the horse. I would never be able to provide room and board for my own animal, so this is the perfect solution.

Thanks for making a dream come true!

Melinda Morrison
